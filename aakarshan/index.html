Introduction

It's a social media platform it lets you connect with your friends online, Originally designed to showcase art work with friends over internet

Purpose

It let anyone with a mail id create account and share media images to everyone on website

Features of Aakarshan

1. Login / Register

  1. Upload Images
  1. Ability to choose Profile cards when registering
  1. Ability to Like, Comment and Share
  1. Notify Users whenever there is a new post
  1. View and Contact all other users
  1. Ability to install as an app on your Device (PWA)

Other Features

  1. Google Material Design (Home Page)
  1. Mobile Responsive
  1. Faster Page Loading (Dynamic Post Loading/ Infinity Scrolling ) Caching and Offline Support (PWA)

Loads in less than 3s on average mobile network speed
